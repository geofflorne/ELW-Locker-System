How do I register a locker?

-Go to the main page of this site and find a locker from the drop down.
-Double check the locker has no lock on it
-Enter your name and click register.
-You will be prompted to enter your UVic email.
-Login to your Uvic email and click the verification link
-Put a locker on the locker asap

Can I register more than one locker?

No, demand is high, so lockers are limited to one per person.

What if I need multiple lockers for a club?

Please use the admin page for special requests

What is the cost?

Free!

Do I have to be in engineering?

No

Do I need to be a student?

No, but you need an @uvic email to register

What if my email does not come?

Give it up to an hour, then talk to an admin to confirm you entered the email you thought. Do not register for a different locker in this time.

How long can I have a locker for?

A single 4 month semester and one renewal for a total of 8 months. After that we will ask you to reregister which may result in you moving to a new locker

Can I keep my locker while on co-op?

If the admin observes that someone away on co-op while still holding a locker, they may ask for release of that locker to accommodate students if there is a high demand

What if I forget my locker number?

Check your original verification email. Otherwise contact an admin.
